Frequently Asked Questions

What is the average delivery time for orders?
The average delivery time for orders depends on various factors, such as the shipping destination, shipping method chosen, and stock availability. Typically, orders are delivered within 3 to 7 business days, but this may vary depending on the circumstances.
How can I track my order?
You can track your order by logging into your account on our website and navigating to the "Order History" section. There, you will find the status of your order, as well as a tracking number that you can use to track your shipment through the shipping carrier's website.
What is your return policy?
Our return policy allows customers to return products within 30 days of receipt for a full refund or exchange. However, the product must be in its original condition and packaging, and the customer is responsible for shipping costs associated with the return. Exceptions to this policy may apply for certain products, so please refer to our website for more information.
